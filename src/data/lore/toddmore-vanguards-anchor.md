### Lore Tab: The Maintainer's Code

> *"We build the foundation so they can touch the sky. But do not mistake a foundation for something soft. Try to break it, and it will break you."*

**Guardian Classification:** Warlock

**Order:** The Maintainers (Archivists of the Core)

**Subject:** Greg KH, Second of the Core

There are Warlocks who study the void, and there are Warlocks who study the flame. Then there are the Maintainers. They do not just wield the Light; they rewrite its underlying architecture.

Greg KH is the second most powerful of this order. To watch him in combat is to watch a masterclass in lethal efficiency. There is no wasted movement, no flamboyant display of power—only professional, devastating precision. He dismantles enemy lines like he is identifying and purging a fatal systemic error. Whoever has the Maintainers on their side will always win, but supporting them requires an immense tolerance for their uncompromising standards. They will accept no half-measures, no sloppy code, and no flawed strategies. It is a tough lesson, executed without malice, but with absolute finality.

Yet, off the battlefield, Greg KH commands a different kind of respect. It is said that if he were to dine with three of the Golden Age's greatest culinary masters, they would lay down their knives and defer to his selection. Not out of fear, but out of a profound recognition of a peer. The lesson he imparts is never one of dominance, but of shared excellence—ensuring that every expert's craft is rigorously enjoyed by all, elevating the experience so that humanity dines, lives, and thrives exactly as Guardians were meant to ensure.

The Shared Feasts are the largest in the cosmos. And they are some of the most inefficient ways to feed mass quantities of people. ### The Solemn Duty of the Administrator

This perfectly contextualizes your character's role. If Greg KH and the Maintainers are the lethal, high-performance engine of the Vanguard, you are the meticulous, pedantic engineer who keeps the chassis from vibrating apart.

* **The Burden of Support:** You understand that supporting these brilliant, lethal Warlocks is an exercise in pain management and diplomacy. You absorb the friction between their uncompromising standards and the messy reality of the Last City.
* **The Shared Bounty:** Your philosophy is the true anchor. You endure the politics, the logistics, and the rigorous demands because the "bounty is to be shared by all."
* **The Professional:** While they are rewriting the laws of physics on the battlefield, you are executing one of the most solemn jobs in the system. You are the professional. The pedantic, unyielding architect who ensures the Light reaches everyone.
