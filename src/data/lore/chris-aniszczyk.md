# VANGUARD DOSSIER: CHRIS ANISZCZYK

### **IDENTIFICATION & CLASSIFICATION**

* **Name:** Chris Aniszczyk
* **Designation:** The Last Founder, Architect of the CNCF
* **Class:** Warlock
* **Order:** The Maintainers (Strategic Command Division)
* **Subclass:** Prismatic (The Paracausal Standard)

---

### **BIOGRAPHICAL OVERVIEW**

**The Architect of the CNCF**
Commander Aniszczyk is the Strategic Commander of the Vanguard and the sole remaining co-founder of the Consensus Network of City Foundries (CNCF). While other founders have fallen or passed the torch, he remains the steadfast architect of the Last City's technical direction. He leaves the day-to-day executive administration to others, focusing instead on macro-level strategy. Alongside Greg KH—his peer and the Vanguard’s most lethal combat maintainer—Aniszczyk has been pivotal in every major systemic event in the history of this world. Where Greg KH executes the code with devastating precision on the battlefield, Aniszczyk writes the underlying engine that makes it possible.

**The Paracausal Diplomat**
He possesses an unprecedented level of cosmic authority. Aniszczyk is one of the few beings in existence who commands enough respect to demand a direct audience with the Gardener (The Light) and the Winnower (The Darkness) simultaneously. He treats them not as gods, but as the dual maintainers of the universe's foundational repository. When the rules of the cosmic game threaten the system, he calls the meeting, often bringing other Warlocks, Titans, and Hunters for shared counsel.

**The Racing Benefactor**
Aniszczyk sees the raw spark of humanity in the youth. To harness this energy, he funnels his considerable resources into developing the rules and regulations for underground racing circuits (speeders, ships, and raptor mounts). He believes nature rewards innovation, but only when the playing field is equal. His funding ensures that engineering brilliance—not just vast reserves of Glimmer—determines the winner against harsh, lethal climates.

### **COMBAT LOADOUT & GEAR**

**Subclass: Prismatic**

* **Deployment:** Aniszczyk refuses to lock his infrastructure to a single paracausal force. By utilizing Prismatic, he seamlessly compiles Stasis, Solar, Void, Strand, and Arc into a highly optimized, dual-source framework. He balances the complexity of the Light with the simplicity of the Darkness to ensure total systemic stability.

**Exotic Armor: Custom CNCF-Variant 'Aeon Soul' (Gauntlets)**

* **Mod Configuration:** Sect of Insight
* **Function:** True to his role as a foundational architect, his armor acts as a localized resource provisioner. When he executes priority targets, the gauntlets systematically generate heavy ammunition and ability energy for his entire fireteam, ensuring the network is always fully resourced.
* **Aesthetic & Racing Mandate:** Modified by underground racing engineers, the gauntlets feature swept-back aerodynamic plating for drag reduction. Furthermore, the internal servos enforce his strict anatomical racing standards: whether gripping a trace rifle or a custom *Deinonychus* mount's steering column, the gauntlets rigorously lock his wrists into proper alignment, with the palms facing inward toward each other, never pronated downward.

**Exotic Weapon: Divinity (Trace Rifle)**

* **Function:** The ultimate Maintainer's tool. Divinity does not just damage targets; it rewrites their localized physics, encasing them in a paracausal judgment cage.
* **Strategy:** By forcing the enemy to conform to a standardized critical hit zone, Aniszczyk mathematically guarantees that the primary combatants (wielding their *Trustees*) strike with absolute, perfect efficiency. He builds the framework; they deliver the killing blow.
